ALPHA - INFORMATION

Triggers

  • Hover over a tab on the menu bar

Rules

  • The cursor changes into a pointer
  • A black underline is drawn from left to right,creating an underline underneath the menu tab name.
  • The time it takes to draw the line is 0.5 seconds.
  • While hovering, the text changes into black
  • When the mouse leaves the tab and stops hovering, the underline runs left to right and disappears.
