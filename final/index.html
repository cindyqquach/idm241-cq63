IKEA
Microinteraction Enhancement: Final

FINAL - INFORMATION

Triggers

    Microinteraction 1

  • Click on the Hamburger menu button

    • Microinteraction 2 and 3

  • Hover over, off and click on the ‘Online store: ikea.us’ button

    • Microinteraction 4

  • Hover over and off the ‘Go shopping‘ button

    • Microinteraction 5

  • Hover over and off links below ‘Other IKEA sites‘

Rules

Microinteraction 1

  • The hamburger menu page slides from the right to left, expanding to cover the entire page.

Microinteraction 2 and 3

1. Hover over

  • The cursor changes into a pointer
  • When the mouse hovers over the 'Online store: ikea.us' button, its background fades into a light yellow.

2. When clicked

  • When clicked, the caret turns upside down and the button moves downward, making room for the 'Find online store' search tool.
  • The 'Find online store' search tool moves up from the bottom, underneath the 'Online store: Ikea.us' button.
  • The text 'Go shopping' moves from the middle up to the top of the box.

3. Hover off

  • When hovering off, the button's background changes back to yellow.

Microinteraction 4

1. Hover over

  • The cursor changes into a pointer
  • When the mouse hovers over the 'Go shopping' button, the arrow icon next to the text floats to the right

2. Hover off

  • When hovering off, the arrow icon next to the 'Go shopping' button floats to the left, back to the original position

Microinteraction 5

1. Hover over

  • The cursor changes into a pointer
  • A black underline is drawn underneath the menu tab name.

2. Hover off

  • When the mouse leaves the tab and stops hovering, the underline disappears.
